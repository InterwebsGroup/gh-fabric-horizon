- The point: To COVER you, not to FIT you

## Writing Guidelines Summary

### Do This
- Lead with benefits, not features
- Use customer language ("cozy hug" not "premium fleece")
- Be specific about numbers (225,000 customers, 7,000 designs, $20 savings)
- Write short paragraphs and sentences
- Include personality (emojis, casual phrases) where appropriate

### Don't Do This
- Don't repeat the same CTA more than twice on a page
- Don't use ALL CAPS except for very short emphasis
- Don't make claims without backing them up
- Don't hedge ("might," "could," "perhaps") â€” be confident
- Don't use industry jargon
